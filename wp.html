About Aqua Escape

Haryana's Premier Water Park Destination

Located in the heart of Ramgarh, Aqua Escape is the region's most exciting water park, offering a perfect blend of thrilling rides and relaxing attractions for visitors of all ages.

Our mission is to provide a safe, clean, and unforgettable aquatic experience that brings families and friends together for moments of joy and excitement.
